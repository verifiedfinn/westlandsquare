About

Westland Square building has a manned reception desk in the afternoons, but is accessed with the use of swipe cards the rest of the time.
It contains 4 storeys and is largely used by students, post graduates with some staff offices on the ground floor and second floor, and a number of seminar rooms.
There is also a canteen for students and a separate one for staff on the first floor.
